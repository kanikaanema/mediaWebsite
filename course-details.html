Bachelor's Program

Bachelors In computer Applications (BCA)

Duration : The duration of a BCA program is typically three years, divided into six semesters.

Eligibility : Candidates who have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) education from a recognized board are eligible for BCA.

Minimum Percentage: Percentage requirement can vary but is often around 45-50% in the aggregate of the 10+2 exams.


Bachelors In Commerce In Computer Applications (B.Com(CA))

Duration : The duration of a BCA program is typically three years, divided into six semesters.

Eligibility : Candidates who have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) with commerce stream from a recognized board are eligible for BCOM(CA).

Minimum Percentage: Percentage requirement can vary but is often around 45-50% in the aggregate of the 10+2 exams.
