About us

Learning with Love and Laughter

Media Computer Education was established on 05 April 2002. The purpose of establishment of Media Computer was to provide high level computer education in Ganjbasoda city itself, so that the students who could not go out to get important education like computer due to being financially weak, are also not deprived of computer education. Today, keeping its objective in mind, the organization is touching new dimensions in the field of education year after year. Where the organization started Media Computer with a small 10 X 10 room and one computer. Today the institution is known as the largest campus of the university. Today the institution has all the IT resources of the university courses.

Kamlesh Sharma, Director

College's Journey

Media Computer Education is a renowned institution that has been on a remarkable journey, transforming countless lives through high-quality computer education. Established in Ganj Basoda City, our institution has been affiliated with the esteemed Makhanlal Chaturvedi University With several years of dedicated service in the field, we have become the go-to destination for students who are financially constrained but aspire to excel in the IT sector.

Purpose And Goals

Our primary purpose at Media Computer Education is to bridge the gap in computer education for those students who are unable to access it due to financial limitations. Our goal is to empower these students with comprehensive IT courses, enabling them to secure lucrative job opportunities and build successful careers in the ever-evolving technology industry.

Introduction to the Team

Behind the success of Media Computer Education stands a team of highly skilled and experienced professionals, who are passionate about empowering students through education. Our faculty members are well-versed in the latest technologies and trends. They strive to provide a supportive and stimulating learning environment, where students can unleash their potential and sharpen their IT skills under their expert guidance.

Offerings

At Media Computer Education, we proudly offer a wide range of courses, which are aligned with the curriculum of Makhanlal Chaturvedi University. Students can choose from various programs, including: (B.C.A), (B.Com(CA)), (msc(CS)), (PGDCA) and (D.C.A). All our courses are designed to be job-oriented, focusing on practical knowledge and skill development. We ensure that our students are equipped with the necessary expertise to excel in their chosen IT career paths.
